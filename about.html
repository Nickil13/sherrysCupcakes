About

Sherry's Cupcakes was established in 2000 by Sherry Zucker. It began as a small cupcake bakery in downtown Calgary and has since grown into a local favourite.

Sherry has been baking ever since she was a little girl. She grew up and became a pastry chef, specializing in the her favourite dessert: cupcakes!
